The Apartment
The Saphire is most amazing apartment of the Plateau : 8 bedrooms on 2 floors, many terraces, a huge kitchen, and an amazing location. The 8 bedrooms are spacious, have windows, and are furnished with a double bed, a desk, a desk lamp, a wardrobe or a large closet. The kitchen is furnished with stove, 2 fridges, micro-wave oven, and a huge table to let you all eat at the same time!
The Neighborhood
The Saphire is located at the heart of the Plateau, 100 meters from Prince Arthur Street and St-Laurent, two of the busiest streets of the Plateau. On Prince Arthur and St-Laurent, you can find many restaurants, bars and clubs! You will be only 1 minute from the metro (Sherbrooke Station/Orange line) that let you go easily to UdeM campus (HEC/UdeM/Polytechnique), in 20 minutes, or to UQAM/ETS in 5 minutes, and a few meters from the bus 24 which takes you to McGill and Concordia! A lot of convenient stores are close by too.
